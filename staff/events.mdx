## üî• Fire Drill Planning

**Monthly Requirements:**
- **Schedule:** First Tuesday of each month
- **Timing:** Vary times to test all scenarios
- **Weather:** Indoor alternatives for severe weather
- **Documentation:** Response times and issues

**Drill Execution:**
1. **Pre-Drill:** Staff notification and preparation
2. **Activation:** Alarm system activation
3. **Monitoring:** Staff observe and time responses
4. **Debrief:** Immediate feedback and improvements

## üîí Lockdown Drill Planning

**Quarterly Requirements:**
- **Scenarios:** Vary threat types and locations
- **Coordination:** Work with local law enforcement
- **Training:** Staff and student preparation
- **Evaluation:** Response effectiveness assessment

**Safety Considerations:**
- **Trauma-Informed:** Age-appropriate approaches
- **Special Needs:** Accommodation planning
- **Communication:** Clear expectations and debriefs
- **Support:** Counseling resources available

---

## üìä Drill Evaluation

**Performance Metrics:**
- **Response Time:** Building evacuation under 5 minutes
- **Accountability:** 100% student/staff accounted for
- **Procedures:** Correct protocol following
- **Communication:** Clear and effective messaging

**Improvement Process:**
1. **Data Collection:** Time, observe, document
2. **Analysis:** Identify strengths and weaknesses
3. **Planning:** Develop improvement strategies
4. **Implementation:** Update procedures and training
5. **Follow-up:** Monitor improvement in next drill
