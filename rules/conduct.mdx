## ‚öñÔ∏è Consequences for Violations

### Progressive Discipline System

**Minor Violations:**
1. **First offense:** Verbal reminder and education
2. **Second offense:** Brief timeout (5-10 minutes)
3. **Third offense:** Extended timeout (15-30 minutes)
4. **Continued violations:** Escalation to major violation

**Major Violations:**
1. **First offense:** Significant timeout + coaching session
2. **Second offense:** Extended suspension + staff meeting
3. **Third offense:** Long-term suspension + review
4. **Continued violations:** Consideration for permanent removal

**Zero Tolerance Violations:**
- **Immediate removal** from current session
- **Automatic suspension** (24+ hours minimum)
- **Staff review required** for return
- **Possible permanent ban** for severe violations

### Appeals Process

**Right to Appeal:**
- Contact staff within 24 hours of consequence
- Provide respectful explanation of your perspective
- Acknowledge any mistakes or poor judgment
- Demonstrate understanding of proper conduct

**Appeal Guidelines:**
- Be honest and take responsibility
- Show genuine desire to improve
- Respect the appeals process and decision
- Use the experience as a learning opportunity
