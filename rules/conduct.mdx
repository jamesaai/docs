## üìã Behavioral Expectations

### During Normal Activities

**Positive Participation:**
- Engage actively in learning activities
- Show interest in emergency preparedness
- Participate in discussions and training
- Help maintain a positive atmosphere

**Respectful Behavior:**
- Follow school rules and expectations
- Use appropriate volume and language
- Respect personal space and boundaries
- Include others in activities and conversations

### During Emergency Situations

**Serious and Focused:**
- Treat all emergencies as real situations
- Follow procedures quickly and correctly
- Help others without being asked
- Stay calm and encourage others

**Supportive Leadership:**
- Take initiative to help when appropriate
- Guide others who may be confused or scared
- Share your knowledge to help everyone succeed
- Model excellent behavior for newcomers

## üéØ Following Instructions

### Staff Authority

**Immediate Compliance:**
- Follow all staff instructions without delay
- Ask questions only when safe and appropriate
- Trust staff decisions during emergencies
- Support staff authority with other players

**Respectful Questioning:**
- "Could you please explain that procedure again?"
- "I want to make sure I understand correctly..."
- "Is there a specific reason we do it this way?"
- "Thank you for the clarification."

### Peer Leadership

**Supporting Others:**
- Help newer players understand expectations
- Encourage good behavior in others
- Report serious conduct violations to staff
- Model excellent behavior consistently

**Positive Influence:**
- Lead by example in all situations
- Encourage others to follow rules and procedures
- Help resolve minor conflicts peacefully
- Contribute to a positive community culture

## ‚öñÔ∏è Consequences for Violations

### Progressive Discipline System

**Minor Violations:**
1. **First offense:** Verbal reminder and education
2. **Second offense:** Brief timeout (5-10 minutes)
3. **Third offense:** Extended timeout (15-30 minutes)
4. **Continued violations:** Escalation to major violation

**Major Violations:**
1. **First offense:** Significant timeout + coaching session
2. **Second offense:** Extended suspension + staff meeting
3. **Third offense:** Long-term suspension + review
4. **Continued violations:** Consideration for permanent removal

**Zero Tolerance Violations:**
- **Immediate removal** from current session
- **Automatic suspension** (24+ hours minimum)
- **Staff review required** for return
- **Possible permanent ban** for severe violations

### Appeals Process

**Right to Appeal:**
- Contact staff within 24 hours of consequence
- Provide respectful explanation of your perspective
- Acknowledge any mistakes or poor judgment
- Demonstrate understanding of proper conduct

**Appeal Guidelines:**
- Be honest and take responsibility
- Show genuine desire to improve
- Respect the appeals process and decision
- Use the experience as a learning opportunity
