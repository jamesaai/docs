## üî• Fire Alarm Rules

### Immediate Response Rules

<Warning>
**Rule #1:** When the fire alarm sounds, you MUST evacuate immediately. No exceptions.
</Warning>

1. **Stop everything immediately**
   - Do not finish conversations
   - Do not collect belongings
   - Do not "wait a second" for anything
   - **Violation:** Major infraction - immediate consequences

2. **Follow evacuation procedures exactly**
   - Use designated evacuation routes
   - Walk quickly but do not run
   - Stay with your group
   - **Violation:** Safety violation - disciplinary action

3. **No trolling or joking during fire alarms**
   - Fire emergencies are serious situations
   - Inappropriate behavior endangers lives
   - This includes "fake" emergencies or false alarms
   - **Violation:** Major infraction - possible removal from game

### Assembly Point Rules

4. **Report to assembly points immediately**
   - Go directly to your assigned assembly area
   - Do not stop to talk to friends
   - Do not leave the assembly area
   - **Violation:** Safety violation - disciplinary action

5. **Remain quiet during attendance**
   - Staff must account for everyone
   - Talking interferes with safety procedures
   - Listen for important announcements
   - **Violation:** Minor infraction - warning/timeout

6. **Do not re-enter the building**
   - Only fire department can declare building safe
   - Re-entering without permission is extremely dangerous
   - Wait for official all-clear announcement
   - **Violation:** Major infraction - immediate removal

## üîí Lockdown Rules

### Immediate Response Rules

<Warning>
**Rule #1:** When lockdown is announced, you MUST secure your area immediately and remain silent.
</Warning>

7. **Secure your location immediately**
   - Lock doors, turn off lights, hide from view
   - Move to designated safe areas
   - Help others secure the area
   - **Violation:** Safety violation - major consequences

8. **Maintain absolute silence**
   - No talking, whispering, or unnecessary movement
   - Turn off electronic devices
   - Control breathing and stay calm
   - **Violation:** Safety violation - disciplinary action

9. **Do not leave safe areas**
   - Stay in your secured location until all-clear
   - Do not peek out windows or doors
   - Do not attempt to "investigate" the situation
   - **Violation:** Major infraction - immediate consequences

### Communication Rules During Lockdown

10. **Emergency communication only**
    - Use chat only for life-threatening emergencies
    - Report injuries or immediate dangers
    - Do not ask questions or speculate
    - **Violation:** Communication violation - timeout

11. **No social media or external communication**
    - Do not post about ongoing lockdown
    - Do not call/text family during active threat
    - Wait for official information
    - **Violation:** Major infraction - disciplinary action

## üì¢ Communication Rules

### Emergency Chat Protocol

12. **Emergency communications take priority**
    - Report actual emergencies immediately
    - Keep messages brief and factual
    - Do not spam emergency channels
    - **Violation:** Communication violation - timeout

13. **No speculation or rumors**
    - Only share confirmed, factual information
    - Do not spread rumors about emergencies
    - Direct questions to staff members
    - **Violation:** Minor infraction - warning

14. **Follow staff instructions immediately**
    - Staff instructions override all other considerations
    - Do not argue or question during emergencies
    - Ask for clarification only if safe to do so
    - **Violation:** Insubordination - disciplinary action

## ‚öñÔ∏è Consequences System

### Violation Levels

**Minor Infraction:**
- First offense: Verbal warning
- Second offense: 5-minute timeout
- Third offense: 15-minute timeout
- Fourth offense: Escalates to major infraction

**Major Infraction:**
- First offense: 30-minute timeout + coaching
- Second offense: 1-hour timeout + staff meeting
- Third offense: 24-hour suspension
- Fourth offense: Permanent removal consideration

**Zero Tolerance Violation:**
- Immediate removal from current session
- 24-hour minimum suspension
- Staff review required for return
- Possible permanent removal

### Appeals Process

18. **Right to appeal consequences**
    - Contact staff within 24 hours of consequence
    - Provide your perspective on the incident
    - Appeals reviewed by senior staff
    - Decision final after appeal review

**Appeal Guidelines:**
- Be respectful and factual
- Acknowledge any mistakes made
- Explain circumstances if relevant
- Accept responsibility for actions

## üí° Tips for Success

### Before Emergencies

- **Study the procedures** - know them by heart
- **Practice during drills** - take every drill seriously
- **Know the building layout** - exits, safe rooms, assembly points
- **Understand your role** - what you should do in each scenario

### During Emergencies

- **Stay calm** - panic helps no one
- **Follow procedures** - they're designed to keep you safe
- **Help others** - work as a team
- **Listen carefully** - important information comes quickly

### After Emergencies

- **Participate in debriefs** - help improve procedures
- **Learn from experience** - what went well, what didn't
- **Support others** - some may be shaken by the experience
- **Practice more** - repetition builds competence
