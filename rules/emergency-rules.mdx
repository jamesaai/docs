    <Warning>
    **Rule #1:** When lockdown announced, secure area IMMEDIATELY and stay silent.
    </Warning>

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Immediate Actions" icon="lock">
        **Must Do:**
        - Lock doors, turn off lights
        - Hide from view immediately
        - Maintain absolute silence
        - Help others secure area

        **Violation:** Safety violation ‚Üí major consequences
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Communication Rules" icon="message-circle">
        **Emergency Chat Only:**
        - Report injuries/immediate dangers
        - No questions or speculation
        - No social media posting
        - No external communication

        **Violation:** Communication violation ‚Üí timeout
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Stay Hidden" icon="eye-off">
        **Requirements:**
        - Don't leave safe areas
        - Don't peek out windows
        - Don't investigate sounds
        - Wait for official all-clear

        **Violation:** Major infraction ‚Üí immediate consequences
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
