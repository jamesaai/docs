## üè´ School Setting Guidelines

### Stay in Character

<Info>
**Remember:** You are a student or staff member in a realistic school setting, not a Roblox player in a game.

**Character Consistency:**
- Maintain your role throughout the session
- Speak and act like a real student/staff member

- Respond realistically to situations

**Appropriate Behavior:**
- Follow normal school social dynamics
- Show respect for authority figures

**Emergency Situations:**
- Show genuine concern during alarms
- Follow procedures like your life depends on it
- Help others as you would in real emergencies
- Display appropriate emotions (concern, urgency, relief)

**Daily School Life:**
- Participate in classes and activities
- Interact naturally with other students
- Follow school rules and expectations
- Show interest in learning and safety

## üí¨ Language Standards

### Appropriate Communication

**School-Appropriate Language:**
- Use language suitable for ages 10+
- Avoid profanity, inappropriate topics
- Speak respectfully to all players

- "Excuse me, teacher, may I use the restroom?"
- "The fire alarm is going off - we need to evacuate!"

## üö® Emergency Roleplay

### During Fire Alarms

**Realistic Student Responses:**
- Initial surprise: "What's that sound?"
- Urgency: "We need to get out now!"
- Helping others: "Stay with me, we'll go together."
- Following procedures: "Remember, we go to the parking lot."

**Realistic Staff Responses:**
- Taking charge: "Everyone line up at the door."
- Giving instructions: "Walk quickly, don't run."
- Checking on students: "Is everyone okay?"
- Coordinating: "I'll take attendance at the assembly point."

### During Lockdowns

**Appropriate Reactions:**
- Concern: "What's happening? Are we safe?"
- Following procedures: "Everyone get away from the windows."
- Quiet cooperation: Whispering or staying silent
- Supporting others: Comforting scared classmates

**Emotional Responses:**
- Show appropriate fear or concern
- Demonstrate how to stay calm under pressure
- Help others manage their emotions
- Express relief when all-clear is given

## üí° Roleplay Tips

### Getting Started

**For New Players:**

- Ask questions about procedures
- Start with simple interactions

**Character Creation:**

- Decide on personality traits

- Think about goals and motivations

## üö´ Roleplay Violations

### Breaking Character

**Avoid These Actions:**
- Talking about the game as a game
- Using modern slang excessively
- Ignoring the school setting
- Not taking emergencies seriously

**Consequences:**
- First offense: Gentle reminder
- Repeated offenses: Roleplay coaching
- Serious violations: Timeout or removal

### Inappropriate Roleplay

**Unacceptable Behaviors:**
- Romantic or inappropriate content
- Violent or aggressive behavior
- Disruptive or attention-seeking actions
- Discrimination or harassment

**Zero Tolerance:**
- Immediate removal from session
- Review by staff team
- Possible permanent suspension
