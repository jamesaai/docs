  <Tab title="‚öôÔ∏è Creation Steps">
    <Warning>
    **Permissions Required:** You must have the **Workspace Admin** permission to create or modify sessions.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Settings">
        Navigate to your dashboard and go to **Workspace Settings > Sessions**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Initiate Creation">
        Click the **Add Session** button located on the top right. A configuration popup will appear.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure Details">
        Fill in the required fields (Name, Description, Time) and select which Role Groups are visible.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Finalize">
        Save your session. It is now ready to be hosted or scheduled.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

## üöÄ Session Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Naming">
    Use distinct names like "Basic Training - 5 PM" rather than just "Training" to avoid confusion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Role Discipline">
    Ensure you only enable role groups that are actually required for the session to prevent overcrowding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regular Audits">
    Review your Session Settings monthly to remove outdated event types or update descriptions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Automation:** Properly configured sessions work seamlessly with Discord webhooks to announce events automatically!
</Tip>
