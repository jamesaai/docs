# üì¢ Emergency Communications

> **Critical Communication:** Clear communication saves lives during emergencies.

---

## üîä PA Announcements

**Fire Emergency:**
- **Continuous alarm** = Evacuate immediately
- **Intermittent beeping** = Standby for instructions
- **Voice announcement** = Follow specific directions

**Lockdown Emergency:**
- **"Lockdown, lockdown, lockdown"** = Secure areas immediately
- **"Secure the building"** = Lock exterior doors, continue activities

**All-Clear:**
- **"All clear"** = Emergency over, resume normal activities
- **Repeated 3 times** = Confirmed by staff verification

**‚ùå Prohibited:**
- "What's happening?"
- "Is this real or a drill?"
- Social conversations
- Speculation about emergency

**Priority Order:**
1. **EMERGENCY** - Life-threatening situations
2. **URGENT** - Route problems, missing persons
3. **IMPORTANT** - Status updates, coordination
4. **ROUTINE** - Suspended during emergencies

---

## üìû Communication Chain

**Emergency Coordinator:** Overall status, building-wide instructions
**Teachers/Staff:** Classroom status, student accountability  
**Students:** Personal emergencies only, follow instructions

**When to Call 911:**
- Someone seriously injured
- Immediate danger to life
- Fire or explosion
- Medical emergency

**Parent Communication:**
- Handled by administration only
- Official channels only
- No individual staff contact during emergencies

---

- **Listen carefully** to all PA announcements
- **Use chat only** for critical safety information
- **Follow chain of command** for all communications
- **Stay calm** and speak clearly during emergencies
