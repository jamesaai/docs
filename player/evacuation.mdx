  <Tab title="ðŸ”¥ Fire Evacuation">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Immediate Response (0-15 seconds)">
        <Warning>**Critical:** Every second counts during fire emergencies</Warning>
        
        - **Stop** all activity immediately
        - **Listen** for alarm type and announcements  
        - **Alert** others who may not have heard
        - **Move** toward nearest exit (no elevators!)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Choose Your Route (15-30 seconds)">
        **Route Priority:**
        1. **Primary:** Nearest designated exit
        2. **Secondary:** Next nearest if blocked
        3. **Emergency:** Any safe exit available
        
        **Never attempt blocked or dangerous routes**
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Move Efficiently (30 seconds - 3 minutes)">
        - **Walk quickly** but don't run
        - **Stay right** in hallways and stairwells
        - **Help others** without stopping traffic
        - **Stay low** if smoke is present
        - **Test doors** before opening (hot = find alternate)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Reach Safety (3-5 minutes)">
        - **Exit building** completely
        - **Move 150+ feet** from building
        - **Go to assembly point** immediately
        - **Find your group** and report missing persons
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  <Tab title="ðŸ”’ Lockdown vs Evacuation">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="When to Evacuate" icon="door-open">
        **Evacuate When:**
        - Fire alarm sounds (even during lockdown)
        - Specifically instructed by law enforcement
        - Your safe room is compromised
        - Directed by emergency responders
      </Card>

      <Card title="When to Stay in Lockdown" icon="lock">
        **Stay When:**
        - No specific evacuation order
        - Uncertain about threat location
        - Safe room is secure and concealed
        - Moving would expose you to danger
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Warning>
    **Fire Priority:** If fire alarm sounds during lockdown, evacuate immediately - fire takes priority
    </Warning>
