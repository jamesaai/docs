        - Ask questions after, not during

  <Tab title="üö® Emergency Procedures">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Fire alarms vs lockdowns - what's the difference?" icon="fire">
        **Key differences:**
        - **Fire Alarms:** Evacuate building ‚Üí go to assembly points
        - **Lockdowns:** Secure location ‚Üí hide, stay silent
        - **Fire takes priority:** If fire alarm sounds during lockdown, evacuate
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="What if I'm in the bathroom during an emergency?" icon="door-closed">
        **Emergency bathroom procedures:**
        - **Fire alarm:** Exit immediately, join nearest evacuation route
        - **Lockdown:** Lock door if possible, stay in stall, remain quiet
        - **Don't flush or run water** unnecessarily during lockdown
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="What if I get separated from my group?" icon="users">
        **If separated during evacuation:**
        1. Continue to nearest safe exit - don't go back
        2. Report to assembly point your exit leads to
        3. Tell staff you were separated from original group
        4. Staff will help reunite you and account for everyone
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="üìã Rules & Conduct">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="What happens if I break a rule accidentally?" icon="info">
        **Accidental violations:**
        - **Minor mistakes:** Usually result in friendly reminders
        - **Show you're learning:** Accept feedback positively  
        - **Ask questions:** If unsure about rules, ask staff
        - **Most violations are learning opportunities,** not punishments
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Can I appeal a consequence I think was unfair?" icon="scale">
        **Yes, you have the right to appeal:**
        1. Contact staff within 24 hours
        2. Explain your perspective respectfully
        3. Acknowledge any mistakes you made
        4. Appeals reviewed by senior staff
        5. Accept final decision gracefully
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="What's considered 'trolling' during emergencies?" icon="ban">
        **Emergency trolling includes:**
        - Making jokes during fire alarms or lockdowns
        - Intentionally not following procedures
        - Disrupting others' emergency responses
        - Treating emergencies like a game
        - **Results in immediate consequences**
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="üè´ Building & Navigation">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do I learn all the exits and safe rooms?" icon="map">
        **Building mastery tips:**
        1. Take virtual tours during calm periods
        2. Practice walking different evacuation routes
        3. Study the [School Layout](/player/school-layout) guide
        4. Time yourself on different routes
        5. Ask experienced players to show you around
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="What if my usual exit is blocked?" icon="door-open">
        **Blocked exit procedures:**
        1. **Don't panic** - buildings have multiple exits
        2. **Use your secondary route** you should have planned
        3. **Follow others** who know alternate routes
        4. **Never try to force through** blocked or dangerous exits
        5. **Go to nearest safe room** if all exits seem blocked
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
