## üöÄ Getting Started

### Your First Session

<Info>
**New Player Priority:** Focus on learning the basics before trying to be a leader or expert.
</Info>

**Before You Start:**
- Read the [Emergency Rules](/rules/emergency-rules) thoroughly
- Study the [School Layout](/player/school-layout) to know exits and safe rooms
- Understand [Roleplay Guidelines](/rules/roleplay) for realistic interaction
- Join our [Discord](https://Discord.gg/firealarm) for community support

**First 10 Minutes:**
- Observe other players and how they interact
- Find a friendly group of students to learn from
- Ask questions politely when you're confused
- Focus on following instructions rather than leading

**Your First Emergency:**
- Stay close to experienced players
- Listen carefully to all announcements
- Follow evacuation routes that others are using
- Don't worry about being perfect - focus on learning

### Character Creation

**Choose Your Role:**
- **New Student:** Perfect for learning, asks lots of questions
- **Transfer Student:** Has some experience, eager to fit in
- **Returning Student:** Knows some procedures, helps newcomers
- **Advanced Student:** Experienced, takes on leadership roles

**Character Personality:**
- **Cautious:** Follows rules carefully, asks for clarification
- **Helpful:** Assists others, shares knowledge freely
- **Leader:** Takes initiative, guides groups during emergencies
- **Curious:** Asks questions, wants to understand everything

## üö® Emergency Response Mastery

### Fire Evacuation Excellence

**Pre-Emergency Awareness:**
- Always know your nearest two exits
- Remember assembly point locations
- Keep pathways clear of obstacles
- Stay alert for alarm signals

**During Fire Alarms:**
- React immediately - no hesitation
- Help others who seem confused
- Stay calm but show appropriate urgency
- Follow evacuation routes efficiently

**Assembly Point Leadership:**
- Help organize your group
- Assist with attendance taking
- Keep others calm and informed
- Report any missing persons immediately

### Lockdown Procedure Mastery

**Preparation:**
- Know safe room locations throughout the building
- Understand different lockdown signals
- Practice moving quickly and quietly
- Remember communication protocols

**During Lockdowns:**
- Secure your area immediately and efficiently
- Help others get to safety
- Maintain absolute silence when required
- Stay alert for further instructions

**Advanced Techniques:**
- Help barricade doors when appropriate
- Assist others with special needs
- Monitor for signs of panic in others
- Be ready to follow additional instructions

## üè´ School Navigation Mastery

### Building Knowledge

**Learn Every Area:**
- **First Floor:** Main entrances, office, cafeteria
- **Second Floor:** Most classrooms, computer lab
- **Third Floor:** Advanced classrooms, art rooms
- **Emergency Routes:** All exits and safe rooms

**Traffic Flow Understanding:**
- Know which routes get crowded during evacuations
- Learn alternate paths when primary routes are blocked
- Understand how different areas evacuate
- Practice moving efficiently in groups

### Advanced Navigation

**Route Optimization:**
- Choose less crowded evacuation routes when safe
- Help direct traffic during busy evacuations
- Know accessibility routes for those with mobility needs
- Understand how different emergencies affect route choices

**Emergency Shortcuts:**
- Learn staff-only routes (when appropriate)
- Know which doors can be used in emergencies
- Understand when to use windows or alternate exits
- Remember that elevators are never used during fire alarms
